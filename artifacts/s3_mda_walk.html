MDA Walk

On March 16th I attended the Muscular Dystrophy walk, I was interested in walking for the MD walk mainly to send kids to camp. We were able to raise enough money to send two kids to camp. Much of the fundraising money goes to research projects, summer camps, MDA equipment program that helps families lend durable medical equipment for those in need of equipment, participating in this event brought me hope that I was part of a bigger piece of the puzzle in helping those who muscular dystrophy have good time at camp, this money went to great causes.
